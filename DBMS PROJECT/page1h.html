Our Mission

At T&N, we believe in the power of exchange. We offer a redemption system that allows users to exchange their sold clothes for coupons valid at prominent brand stores, including Zara and H&M. We aim to foster sustainable practices within the fashion Industry, aligning with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), with a particular focus on responsible consumption and production (SDG 12). We also offer the option of providing cash to customers during the collection process, providing flexibility and catering to individual customer preferences.
